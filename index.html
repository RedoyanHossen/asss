What is Innovation?

The Definition

Innovation is the process of translating an idea or invention into a good or service that creates value or for which customers will pay.

Why It Matters

It drives growth, solves global challenges like climate change, and improves the overall quality of human life across the globe.

The Process

Innovation starts with curiosity, moves through experimentation, and finds success through persistence and rapid prototyping.
